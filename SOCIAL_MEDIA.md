MASTER'S THESIS: ESG Impact on Alpha and Beta (STOXX 600)
Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona, Master in Finance (MUFIB)
June 2026 | GitHub Repository

Analyzed impact of ESG performance on risk-adjusted returns across 
598 STOXX 600 companies (2015-2025, 1.5M+ daily observations).

Methods:
- Carhart 4-Factor Model (H1: Green Premium anomaly)
- Dynamic Panel with Driscoll-Kraay (H2: ESG valuation effects)
- Markov-Switching CAPM (H3: Risk amplification in crisis)

Key Finding: Better ESG companies generate lower returns (-0.60%/month, 
t=-5.2***), but provide relative protection in downturns (+0.21 beta in 
crisis, p=0.0024***). Suggests ESG captures real risk dimension.

Technology: Python (Colab), R (statsmodels), 1,576,944 obs, 
reproducible pipeline with 8 analysis scripts.

Repository: github.com/leabergero/tfm-esg-alpha-beta-stoxx600
Status: Complete analysis, fully documented, publication-ready.
